First steps in the world of gaming

Over the decades, Nintendo has adapted to changes in the entertainment industry. From humble beginnings as a card game manufacturer, the company has broadened its horizons by exploring sectors such as toys, hotels and even taxi-distribution. However, it was in the 1970s that Nintendo entered the world of video games.

The Video Games Era

With the launch of the magnificent Donkey Kong in 1981, the company began to make its mark in video game industry. But that was just the beginning. The introduction of the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) in 1985 changed all that, establishing Nintendo as one of the leading forces driving forces behind the rebirth of the video game market after the crash of 1983.

Reinventing gaming with Wii

The following years were marked by a series of bold innovations. The launch of the Wii in 2006 revolutionised gaming by introducing intuitive motion controls, thus opening up to the world to a wider audience than ever before.

The triumph of the Nintendo 3DS

And then, in 2011, the Nintendo 3DS was born. This portable console has captivated gamers the world the world with its glasses-free 3D display and a library of exciting games. The Nintendo 3DS continued Nintendo's tradition of innovation and solidified the company's position position as the as the undisputed pioneer of the games industry.
